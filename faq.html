Appointments & emergencies

I'm in pain right now. How fast can you see me?
Select "Emergency / tooth pain" in the booking form — your request is flagged the instant you submit and we hold same-day emergency slots every day. Most urgent requests get a callback within minutes during opening hours.
What happens after I submit the form?
Our smart reception system reads your request immediately, sends you an email confirmation, and queues a callback. A real human from our team then phones you to agree a time — you're never booked into anything without speaking to us first.
Can I just phone instead?
Of course — 0161 000 0000 during opening hours. The online form is simply faster outside of hours, and emergencies submitted online are triaged instantly even when the phone line is busy.
What if I need to cancel or rearrange?
Just give us 24 hours' notice and there's never a charge. Life happens — we'd rather rearrange than have you skip the appointment altogether.

Costs & payment

Do you take NHS patients?
Yes — we're currently accepting new NHS patients, and we also offer private plans and 0% finance for cosmetic treatments. Tell us which you'd prefer when reception calls you back.
How much will my treatment cost?
Check-ups start from £45 and every treatment plan comes with a written quote before anything happens — no surprises at the desk. See the treatments page for guide prices.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes — 0% finance is available on any treatment over £300, spread over 6 to 12 months. Longer terms are available for Invisalign and implants. We'll set it up during your consultation; it takes about five minutes.

Nervous patients

I'm nervous about the dentist. Will you judge me?
Never. Around a third of our patients describe themselves as nervous — it's genuinely our speciality. Tell us in the notes when you book and we'll go at your pace, explain everything first, and stop whenever you ask.
It's been years since my last check-up. Is that a problem?
Not at all, and you won't get a lecture. Whatever state your teeth are in, we've seen worse — we'll simply make a plan together and take it one step at a time.
Do you offer sedation?
Yes — for surgical treatments and for patients whose anxiety makes treatment difficult, we offer IV sedation with Dr. Rivera. Mention it when you book and we'll talk you through the options.

Practical bits

Where do I park?
There's paid parking directly on Example Row and the NCP on Major Street is two minutes' walk. We're also two minutes from Piccadilly Gardens for trams and buses.
Is the practice accessible?
Yes — step-free access throughout, a ground-floor surgery for patients with limited mobility, and an accessible bathroom. Let us know anything that would make your visit easier and we'll arrange it.
Can my whole family register?
Please do — we love family bookings and can usually see everyone in one visit. Children are free on the NHS, and we make their first visits short, gentle and genuinely fun.

Still wondering about something?

Ask it in the notes box when you request your appointment — our team will answer when they call you back.
